Daughters Rights in the property

India is the country which provides protection to the rights of its citizens equally. Constitution of India endeavours to set equal circumstances for its citizens for their development. Therefore considering Article 14 of Constitution of India the Hon’ble Supreme Court of India in the matter of Veenita Sharma Vs Rajesh Sharma have given equal right to the daughters in their father’s property.

Hindu Succession Act, 1956

The Parliament brought Hindu Succession Act in the year of 1956 in order to set succession procedure to transfer the property. However, according to this act daughters had not got equal right in the property of her father’s property.

Hindu Succession Act (Amendment 2005)

According to this amendment daughters had got equal birth right on the property of her father but she can claim when her father died after the Amendment act, 2005.

Vineeta Sharma Vs Rajesh Sharma

The Hon’ble Supreme Court of India in the matter of Vineeta Sharma Vs Rajesh Sharma have given equal right to the daughters even though father died before 2005 amendment.

Equal Right and Liabilities

Although daughter can enjoy equal right on his father’s property but she must perform all the duties equally which are expected from the Son.
